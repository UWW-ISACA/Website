What Is ISACA?

ISACA club activities offer hands-on learning experiences where students collaborate to address challenges in the field of information systems. Through workshops, case studies, and group projects, participants develop their skills in areas like IT auditing, cybersecurity, and data privacy. These activities foster teamwork, problem-solving, and critical thinking, preparing students for success in the IT industry.

How Can I Get Involved?

Students are welcome to attend our club meetings, held every Monday from 5-6 PM in Hyland Hall, Room 3106, at no cost. To connect with other members, learn about upcoming events, and engage in discussions related, join our Discord Server

Becoming an ISACA Member

Students interested in the extra benefits ISACA offers and who want to expand their knowledge and involvement in their field and the club are encouraged to become ISACA members.

Benefits

ISACA members receive an official ISACA t-shirt, access to additional learning opportunities and resources, can participate in competitions, and receive Certification training.
